Our Story

In the fall of 2002, we opened the doors to Boston Beans for the first time. Our dream to open up a coffee shop stemed from a desire to create a charming neighborhood cafe that could serve the needs of our community while also supporting the needs of our partners abroad. Our mission is to serve quality coffee that is ethically sourced to improve the lives of our coffee bean farmers' and promote Fair Trade practices in our community.
